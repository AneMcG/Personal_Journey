About anemcgrail

  • Business portraits
  • Outdoor photography
  • Website imagery
  • Aerial photography
  • Real estate photography

I am a photographer living in Masterton, New Zealand.

Growing up I was more interested in doing skids in my go kart then do photography. Riding bikes and playing sports were my hobbies. It was never anything to do with the arts. Later I started playing the guitar and joined a band. I ran through life with very little patients. My parents always had cameras and whenever we went on holiday it was always stopped here, go stand there, "oh that would make a nice photo". I got so frustrated but naturally, this created a bit of interest.

It wasn't till much later in life I discovered the art of photography for myself. The first time I took a photo that wasn't just a picture, it wasn't a quick snap I would later scroll through on my phone. It was a frame that captured a feeling. The depth, the detail, the majesty of a mountain. It was that moment an interest in photography became a passion.

Now I strive to use this passion to help businesses to find their unique visual identity. To capture and display an idea, a moment of inspiration, the hard work and long hours that goes into following a dream.
